Why Meet Your Tribe?

Your Loco-Socio Network

There are like minded people in pretty much every domain. It is our focussed goal to connect all these like minded people to create wonders and to transform this planet for a good cause.

You know how in small towns, everyone knows everyone? That embeds a certain kind of trust in people.

Our goal grows deeper as we plan to introduce you to people in your own locality who think in the same way as you. The idea of connecting everyone with the similar interest also includes showcasing businesses, job profiles, people with the same interests.

Meet Your Tribe Includes the following features:

1. Business Listing:
All the sellers including the Samosa Guy can register with MYT because it is free.He will be displayed on the MYT application.

2. Connecting Like Minded People:
You do not have to search for a person over MYT, all you have to do is to carefully select five interests from over thousands, so that you will be added to a group chat with all the people with the similar interests.

3. Increasing ease of doing business:
Companies can register under the commercial category, and when they post a job it again picks up the interest. The people with the same interest gets notified about the job.
